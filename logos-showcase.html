Logo 1 — Mark + Wordmark

The circle divided into 4 quadrants references the 4D framework directly. The gold top-right quadrant represents "the edge" — the advantage that systematic thinking gives. The crosshairs suggest precision and a framework lens. Scales down to 24×24px as a favicon or social avatar.

Logo 2 — Typographic

The italic Playfair Display creates forward momentum — appropriate for a systematic investor who learns by moving. The "4" in gold and "D" in currentColor create a two-tone mark without an icon. The gold rule and tracked mono "INVESTING" below provide structural contrast. Works best at medium-to-large sizes.
